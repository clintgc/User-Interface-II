My Life's Work on a Single Page.

It’s amazing how a single comment can change the course of one’s life. My life-changing comment came from my high school chemistry teacher who said I would amount to nothing in my life. Well, with the drive—and maybe a chip on my shoulder—to prove him wrong, I’ve become a well-respected entrepreneur who launched my first business after college and has been a key part of multiple revolutionary products, companies, and a successful exit ever since.

While I know I’ve surpassed the expectations of my high school teachers, my drive to succeed, deliver results, and be an innovator and market disrupter has never been higher.

My life in work.
